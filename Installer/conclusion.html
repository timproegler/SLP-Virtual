FAQ

Where is SLP-Virtual located?

SLP-Virtual is an audio driver. You can find it in Audio Midi Setup and within audio application.

Why is nothing playing through SLP-Virtual?

  • Check System Preferences -> Security & Privacy -> Privacy -> Microphone to make sure your audio application has microphone access.
  • Check that the volume is all the way up on BlackHole output in Audio Midi Setup.
  • If you are using a multi-output device, due to issues with macOS a 2ch audio driver must be enabled and listed as the top device in the Multi-Output.

How do I uninstall SLP-Virtual

To uninstall SLP-Virtual drivers, just run this installer package again. Please ignore the error message at the end, the drivers will be uninstalled anyway.
